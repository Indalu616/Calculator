Three years coders

AddisCoder is a free intensive 4-week summer program in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia introducing high schoolers to programming and algorithms. The program ran previously in 2011, 2016, 2018, 2019, and 2023. The next offering of AddisCoder will be July 14 to August 9, 2024.

AddisCoder students are current high schoolers who come from all across Ethiopia for 4 weeks to take the course in Ethiopiaâ€™s capital city, Addis Ababa. In 2011 in the first edition of the course, students registered themselves for the course via e-mail. Since 2016, the program has grown much larger into a collaborative effort between AddisCoder Inc. and the Meles Zenawi Foundation, attracting top students from all regions of Ethiopia with help from the Ministry of Education.
